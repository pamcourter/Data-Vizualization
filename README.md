# Data Vizualization collection
I'm learning how to created informative dashboards in Excel and Power BI. Here I will show my most recent or best vizualizations in excel, power BI and Tableau. 

## Projects 
### Excel Kaggle OnlineRetail.csv 
 -  [Revenue Sales Dashboards](./Revenue_sales_dashboard.png/) My first excel dashboard based on Kaggle OnlineRetail.csv. Here I found that Most sales occur on Tuesday and Thursday with a slight drop on Wednesdays. I also did a histogram to reveal that most orders were in the $200-$450 range with a small spike in large orders over $1000. My actionable recommendation would be sending out a special 10% off up to $45 to target customers in this range.
